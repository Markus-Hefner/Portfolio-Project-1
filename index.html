Welcome to the drum school for our youngest drummers!

Why Kid's Drums

  • Smaller drum kits to better suit the body height of younger children
  • A drum learning system espacially designed for children from 5-10 that makes use of the natural way of learning (besser formulieren)
  • A friendly atmosphere that encourages creativity and play
  • Precise, child-friendly feedback
